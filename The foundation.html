COMMON WEALTH SPORT FOUNDATION

As progressive leaders in sport and social development, the Commonwealth Sport Movement continually strives
to demonstrate the power of sport; to unlock our human potential and transform lives.

Founded as the charitable foundation of the Commonwealth Sport Movement, the Commonwealth Sport
Foundation will look to help address some of the world's greatest challenges.

                "Through the Commonwealth Sport Foundation, and inspired by our engaged 
            athletes and communities, our aim is to shape a more peaceful, sustainable and
                         prosperous future across the Commonwealth."

Dame Louise Martin, CGF President

The Foundation's Development model

A key objective in the Transformation 2022 strategy developed by Commonwealth Sport Movement, the
establishment of the foundation includes five development priorities: Youth Empowerment, Equal Rights,
Historical Injustice, Small States and Island States; and Sustainable Cities & Communities.
